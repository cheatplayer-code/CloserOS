# Research Material

Place original reports, interview notes, screenshots, and provider exports under `research/raw/`.

Rules:

- Raw research is evidence, not a source of truth.
- Do not let an agent copy architecture, legal conclusions, pricing, or provider behavior without verification.
- Summarize accepted conclusions into the relevant document under `docs/`.
- Current provider behavior must be verified against official documentation.
- Legal conclusions require qualified Kazakhstan counsel.
- Never place real customer conversations or personal data here.
- Large PDFs are excluded from automatic indexing by `.cursorindexingignore`; reference them manually only when needed.
